Our Services

Your Trusted Partner for Professional Financial Services

AMK AND COMPANY provides a range of services, including audit, corporate law, taxation, consulting, and business outsourcing.

Auditing

The firm specializes in Statutory, Internal, Management, Due Diligence, and Concurrent Audits, along with Financial Reporting and bank audits.

Internal Auditing

Specializes in Statutory, Management, and Due Diligence Audits, as well as bank audits including Branch, Revenue, and Stock Audits.

Professional Assignments

Expertise in due diligence, accounting systems, financial consultancy, MIS, system design, project reports, loans, and many more.

Income Tax

Expertise in Direct Taxation work including representation before Assessing Officer, CIT (Appeals), and I.T.A.T etc.

Corporate Law

Company incorporation and other Company Law Work including representation before Company Law Board for hearing of petitions.

Accounting Outsourcing

Comprehensive bookkeeping and financial reporting services to let clients focus on their core business.
