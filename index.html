About Us

What we do?

AMK AND COMPANY, Chartered Accountants, was established in 2013 by CA. Manish Khandelwal, who brings over 11 years of rich experience. As the firm has grown, it has evolved into a mid-sized, single-window setup with a dedicated team of about 30 professionals. Each vertical of the firm is led by experts with substantial experience.

The firm operates from four offices located in New Delhi, Noida, Jodhpur, and Rishikesh. Our large office premises feature state-of-the-art infrastructure, including a well-equipped library that keeps staff updated on all relevant changes and developments. We operate in a fully computerized environment to enhance efficiency.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide clients with a comprehensive 'One-Stop Solution' for all their business, financial, and regulatory needs. Our commitment to 'Experience, Expertise, and Efficiency' has enabled us to deliver valuable solutions that meet our clients' requirements.
