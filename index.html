Frequently Asked Questions

What services does AMK AND COMPANY offer?

AMK AND COMPANY offers consultancy services in Indirect Taxation, Direct Taxation, Auditing & Assurance, Corporate Laws, Corporate Advisory Services, Financial Services, Accounting Services, and more. They also provide specialized services such as Company Registration, FEMA Matters, ERP Implementation, and International Taxation.

Where are the firm’s offices located?

AMK AND COMPANY has offices in four cities: New Delhi, Noida, Jodhpur, and Rishikesh. These offices are equipped with modern infrastructure to cater to the firm's business and client needs.

Who makes up the team at AMK AND COMPANY?

The team at AMK AND COMPANY consists of qualified, semi-qualified, and other staff members who are highly trained and motivated. They have a wealth of knowledge and experience in various sectors of finance, taxation, and auditing.

What industries does the firm cater to?

AMK AND COMPANY serves a wide range of industries, supporting Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) and Listed Companies. The firm is also adept at executing multi-locational projects for clients across various sectors.
